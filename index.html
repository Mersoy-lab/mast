i. The Mast

Bind yourself to a system stronger than impulse.

Wake at six. Quit nicotine. Train five times a week. Stop scrolling at 9pm. Whatever it is — bind it before the storm.

ii. The Anti-Charity

Make failure cost something real.

If you break the contract, your money doesn't go to a cause you love. It goes to one you can't stand. Loss aversion is one of the strongest forces in human behavior — Mast points it at your future, not against it.

Calibrated, not cruel. Ethical safeguards. Balanced political neutrality. No targeting of vulnerable groups. The point is friction strong enough to interrupt the impulse — not punishment for its own sake.
